Description

I created this 'My Notes' App based on the course React from scratch (Adam Romański).  The goals of this app is:

  1. 1. Add simple notes (title, and description)
  2. 2. Add note of article with link to article (title of article, link to article and description)
  3. 3. Write the planned projects (title of project, person who is responsible for it, and short description).

Further I want to add more function like dates, login/ log out, form validation, search input and  connect to Mongo DB (create backend).
